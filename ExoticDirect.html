ExoticDirect

Pet Insurance for Birds, Mammals and Reptiles

ExoticDirect have over 20 years’ experience in insuring birds, mammals and reptiles, and are one of the leading exotic pet insurance providers in the United Kingdom. As a registered trade mark of Brooks Braithwaite (Sussex) Ltd we pride ourselves on offering flexible insurance, with a range of cover levels to suit client budget and requirements.

Policies are underwritten by Allianz Insurance plc (the United Kingdom’s largest pet insurance underwriter) and can cover rare, exotic or unusual pets ranging from parrots, bearded dragons and tortoises to more popular domestic pets such as guinea pigs and rabbits. In addition, liability insurance is also available for clients wishing to take animals to public places, such as shows or exhibitions.

Cover can include:

Cover available is dependent on pet type and client requirements.

  • 1 Veterinary Fee cover for accidental injury or illness
  • 2 Mortality
  • 3 Fire, Theft and Weather perils
